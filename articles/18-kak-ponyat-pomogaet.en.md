## Stool Normalization: Key Indicator of Efficacy

**Improved stool in cats** and **improved stool in dogs** is the primary criterion for determining if the probiotic is working.

## For Cats

_Saccharomyces boulardii_ effectively reduces symptoms of inflammatory bowel disease (IBD) in cats, including diarrhea and vomiting. The probiotic is safe for various digestive issues, including diarrhea and constipation, and also helps prevent hairballs in long-haired cats.[[fullbuckethealth](https://www.fullbuckethealth.com/blogs/news/the-benefits-of-using-s-boulardii-for-cats)]

## For Dogs

In dogs with chronic enteropathy, the use of S. boulardii (Enterol) has been confirmed by research to be effective for improving clinical status. After discontinuing other medications, stool became mushy with defecation once daily — an indicator of normalization.[[spbvet](https://spbvet.info/zhurnaly/2-2022/khronicheskaya-enteropatiya-ne-poddayushchayasya-lecheniyu-klinicheskiy-sluchay/)]

Probiotics suppress pathogen growth, improve nutrient absorption, and promote normalization of intestinal function in dogs and cats.[[kovcheg](https://kovcheg.vet/poleznye-sovety/kak-pravilno-davat-probiotiki-sobake-ili-koshke/)]
