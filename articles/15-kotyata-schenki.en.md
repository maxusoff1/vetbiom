# Saccharomyces boulardii in Kittens and Puppies: Scientific Review of Probiotic Use

**Source:** Systematic review based on PubMed/PMC + veterinary clinical guidelines[[pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC12606370/)]

---

## The Sensitive Gut

The gut of newborn **kittens** and **puppies** is in the process of microbiome formation, making it especially sensitive to infections, stress, and diet changes. **S. boulardii for kittens** and **S. boulardii for puppies** is recommended as an effective **probiotic for kittens** and **probiotic for puppies** with a proven mechanism of action.[[cliniciansbrief](https://www.cliniciansbrief.com/article/saccharomyces-boulardii-new-probiotic-approach-veterinary-medicine)]

**Saccharomyces boulardii** is not a bacterial but a yeast probiotic that is resistant to gastric acid, heat, and antibiotics. Unlike bacterial probiotics, it passes through the GI tract unchanged and remains active even during antibiotic therapy.[[thornevet](https://thornevet.com/glossary/saccharomyces-boulardii/)]

The mechanism of action includes:

- Displacement of pathogenic microorganisms from the gut
    
- Neutralization of bacterial toxins
    
- Maintenance of intestinal barrier integrity
    
- Production of short-chain fatty acids that nourish beneficial microflora
    
- Modulation of immune response and reduction of inflammation[[thornevet](https://thornevet.com/glossary/saccharomyces-boulardii/)]
    

Research shows that **S. boulardii** improves intestinal function in farm animals and confirms its safety for dogs and cats.[[pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC12606370/)]

## Dose Caution

Dosing of **probiotic for puppies** and **probiotic for kittens** requires accurate calculation, as overdose can cause discomfort while underdosing reduces efficacy.

**Recommended doses (based on veterinary sources):**

|Animal|Body weight|Dose (Saccharomyces boulardii CNCM I-745)|Frequency|
|---|---|---|---|
|Cats / kittens|Any|250 mg|2 times daily [[belkavet](https://belkavet.ru/lekarstva-dlya-zhivotnyh/probiotiki/enterol.htm)]|
|Dogs / puppies|≤10 kg|250 mg|2 times daily [[belkavet](https://belkavet.ru/lekarstva-dlya-zhivotnyh/probiotiki/enterol.htm)]|
|Dogs / puppies|>10 kg|500 mg|2 times daily [[belkavet](https://belkavet.ru/lekarstva-dlya-zhivotnyh/probiotiki/enterol.htm)]|
|General rule|—|50 mg/kg|2 times daily [[vetprotocol](https://vetprotocol.ru/drug/enterol)]|

The powder can be mixed with food or water; capsules can be opened. Important: the dose should be selected individually by a veterinarian, taking into account the age, weight, and health status of the animal.[[belkavet](https://belkavet.ru/lekarstva-dlya-zhivotnyh/probiotiki/enterol.htm)]

**S. boulardii for kittens** under 8 weeks of age requires special caution — it is recommended to start with half the dose and monitor the reaction.[[belkavet](https://belkavet.ru/lekarstva-dlya-zhivotnyh/probiotiki/enterol.htm)]

## When a Veterinarian Is Needed

Consult a veterinarian in the following cases:

- **Diarrhea lasting more than 24 hours** in a kitten or puppy
    
- **Blood or mucus in the stool**
    
- **Vomiting, refusal to eat, lethargy**
    
- **Signs of dehydration** (dry gums, loss of skin elasticity)
    
- **Condition worsened after starting the probiotic**
    
- **Chronic enterocolitis** — in this case, **S. boulardii for puppies** may be more beneficial than other probiotics, but only under veterinary supervision[[spbvet](https://spbvet.info/zhurnaly/2022/enterokolit-u-shchenka/)]
    

**S. boulardii for puppies** and **probiotic for kittens** is indicated for:

- Dysbiosis and gastroenteritis
    
- Post-antibiotic diarrhea
    
- Diet change or stress (relocation, mating, exhibitions)
    
- Reduced immunity[[belkavet](https://belkavet.ru/lekarstva-dlya-zhivotnyh/probiotiki/enterol.htm)]
    

Veterinary clinical cases confirm the efficacy of **S. boulardii** in chronic enterocolitis in puppies. The probiotic is especially useful during or after antibiotic therapy, for gastrointestinal infections, and periods of digestive upset.[[spbvet](https://spbvet.info/zhurnaly/2022/enterokolit-u-shchenka/)]
