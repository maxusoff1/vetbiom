## Why S. boulardii Is Given Alongside Antibiotics

Antibiotics eliminate not only harmful bacteria but also part of the beneficial intestinal microflora. This often leads to antibiotic-associated diarrhea in animals, impaired digestion, and an increased risk of microbiota imbalance.[[saccharomycesboulardii](https://www.saccharomycesboulardii.com/chto-takoe-saharomicety-bulardi-cncm-i-745/nauchnye-dannye/)]

**Saccharomyces boulardii** is a yeast probiotic that is not a bacterium, so it is not directly suppressed by many antibiotics and can be used concurrently with the treatment course.[[fullbuckethealth](https://www.fullbuckethealth.com/blogs/news/three-reasons-to-use-s-boulardii-for-dogs)]

## How It Helps

S. boulardii supports the gut through multiple mechanisms. It helps reduce the risk of antibiotic-associated diarrhea, supports intestinal barrier function, and aids in restoring a normal GI environment after microbial balance disruption.[[journals.rcsi](https://journals.rcsi.science/0555-1099/article/download/138752/116686)]

Veterinary materials specifically emphasize that S. boulardii yeast possess antimicrobial properties, interfere with pathogen attachment to the intestinal wall, and help protect beneficial microflora during antibiotic therapy.[[vicgroup](https://vicgroup.ru/publications/effektivnost-primeneniya-kormovykh-dobavok-na-osnove-saccharomyces-boulardii-v-ratsionakh-sviney/)]

## Why This Matters in Animals

In animals, the gut often reacts to antibiotics not only with diarrhea but also with reduced appetite, impaired feed absorption, and general weakening. This is especially noticeable in young animals, animals after stress, during diet change, and during treatment of infections.[[pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/37576772/)]

Thus, S. boulardii is used not as a replacement for antibiotics but as **concurrent support**: it helps reduce undesirable intestinal effects of treatment and speeds the return to normal GI function.[[saccharomycesboulardii](https://www.saccharomycesboulardii.com/chto-takoe-saharomicety-bulardi-cncm-i-745/nauchnye-dannye/)]

## What the Literature Shows

A source dedicated to scientific data on S. boulardii CNCM I-745 directly states that this strain has been studied for the prevention of antibiotic-associated diarrhea and recovery from intestinal disorders.[[saccharomycesboulardii](https://www.saccharomycesboulardii.com/chto-takoe-saharomicety-bulardi-cncm-i-745/nauchnye-dannye/)]

A review of probiotic properties of saccharomycetes also notes the efficacy of S. boulardii in antibiotic-related diarrhea and several other intestinal disorders.[[journals.rcsi](https://journals.rcsi.science/0555-1099/article/download/138752/116686)]

Veterinary and applied materials on dogs and farm animals indicate that S. boulardii can be used simultaneously with antibiotics since it helps protect beneficial bacteria and reduces the likelihood of stool disruption.[[vicgroup](https://vicgroup.ru/publications/effektivnost-primeneniya-kormovykh-dobavok-na-osnove-saccharomyces-boulardii-v-ratsionakh-sviney/)]

## When It Is Especially Appropriate

S. boulardii is particularly useful when antibiotics are prescribed:

- for intestinal infections;
    
- for animals with a sensitive GI tract;
    
- in young animals;
    
- for long antibiotic courses;
    
- if there has been previous diarrhea during treatment.[[pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/37576772/)]
    

## Important Limitations

S. boulardii does not replace the need for proper antibiotic selection and does not itself treat the cause of infection. If the animal has blood in the stool, severe lethargy, vomiting, or dehydration, veterinary diagnostics are required — not just a probiotic.[[journals.rcsi](https://journals.rcsi.science/0555-1099/article/download/138752/116686)]

It should also be noted that efficacy depends on the specific strain, dosage, and product form; scientific data are most convincing for individual studied strains, not for any supplement with a similar name.[[saccharomycesboulardii](https://www.saccharomycesboulardii.com/chto-takoe-saharomicety-bulardi-cncm-i-745/nauchnye-dannye/)]

## Conclusion

S. boulardii is given alongside antibiotics because it helps reduce the risk of antibiotic-associated diarrhea, supports the intestinal barrier, and helps maintain microflora balance during treatment.[[fullbuckethealth](https://www.fullbuckethealth.com/blogs/news/three-reasons-to-use-s-boulardii-for-dogs)]  
For animals, this is a useful support, especially if the GI tract is sensitive or the antibiotic course is long.[[vicgroup](https://vicgroup.ru/publications/effektivnost-primeneniya-kormovykh-dobavok-na-osnove-saccharomyces-boulardii-v-ratsionakh-sviney/)]
