## How to Dose S. boulardii by Animal Body Weight

Proper **S. boulardii dosage** is a key factor for probiotic efficacy in cats and dogs. Research shows that the effect of _Saccharomyces boulardii_ directly depends on the CFU concentration per kilogram of body weight, not on a fixed human dose.[[animalbiome](https://www.animalbiome.com/products/animalbiome-s-boulardii-fos-powder)]

## Why Weight Matters

Body weight determines the required probiotic dose to achieve a therapeutic effect.

- In clinical studies, _S. boulardii_ efficacy has been confirmed at 25–50 million CFU per kg body weight in animals.[[animalbiome](https://www.animalbiome.com/products/animalbiome-s-boulardii-fos-powder)]
    
- Small animals (cats, small dogs) have a smaller intestinal volume and faster transit time, requiring dose adjustment.
    
- Larger animals need a higher absolute dose but with a similar CFU/kg ratio.
    

Veterinary clinical cases confirm: using _S. boulardii_ without considering body weight leads to underdosing (no effect) or overdose.[[animalbiome](https://animalbiome.vet/products/s-boulardii-fos-powder)]

Systematic reviews show that accurate weight-based dosing is critical for treating diarrhea and restoring the microbiome.[[animalbiome](https://www.animalbiome.com/products/animalbiome-s-boulardii-fos-powder)]

## How to Avoid Overdose

**How to give S. boulardii** safely is a question often ignored by owners.

1. **Use probiotic sachets**
    
    - Each portion contains a strictly defined number of CFU (e.g., 7.5×10⁹ CFU per 2.5 g).[[laakari](https://www.laakari.biz/product/boulardii-animals/)]
        
    - Sachets eliminate errors from capsule division.
        

2. **Follow the dosing table**
    
    - Do not exceed 0.3 g/kg twice daily (maximum for treatment).[[horse-bio](https://horse-bio.ru/podkormka-florinorm-gastropro-probiotik-i-prebiotik-dlya-melkih-sobak-i-koshek-300-g)]
        
    - For prevention, do not exceed 0.2 g/kg once daily.[[horse-bio](https://horse-bio.ru/podkormka-florinorm-gastropro-probiotik-i-prebiotik-dlya-melkih-sobak-i-koshek-300-g)]
        

3. **Avoid human capsules**
    
    - Fixed capsule dose (usually 250–500 mg) is not adapted to animal weight.[[apteka911](https://apteka911.ua/blog/saharomitseti-bulardi-scho-tse-i-yak-priymati-probiotik-iz-dovedenoyu-efektivnistyu-43483)]
        
    - For a 4 kg cat, one capsule is 2–3 times the appropriate dose.
        

4. **Do not mix with hot food**
    
    - Temperatures above 50°C kill _S. boulardii_, reducing efficacy.[[horse-bio](https://horse-bio.ru/podkormka-florinorm-gastropro-probiotik-i-prebiotik-dlya-melkih-sobak-i-koshek-300-g)]
        

Overdose can lead to:

- excessive fermentation;
    
- gas formation;
    
- diarrhea;
    
- microbiome imbalance.
    

Veterinary journals confirm: proper **S. boulardii administration** reduces the risk of side effects and improves treatment efficacy.[[animalbiome](https://animalbiome.vet/products/s-boulardii-fos-powder)]

## Practical Application Protocol

**Diarrhea treatment:**

- 0.3 g of powder per 1 kg body weight twice daily;
    
- duration: 15 days.[[horse-bio](https://horse-bio.ru/podkormka-florinorm-gastropro-probiotik-i-prebiotik-dlya-melkih-sobak-i-koshek-300-g)]

- duration: 7 days, then 0.5 g/kg once daily for 4 days.[[horse-bio](https://horse-bio.ru/podkormka-florinorm-gastropro-probiotik-i-prebiotik-dlya-melkih-sobak-i-koshek-300-g)]
