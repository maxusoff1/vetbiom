## S. boulardii + FOS: Why the Combination Is Needed

The **S. boulardii FOS** combination is becoming the standard in modern veterinary probiotics for cats and dogs. Scientific research confirms that the combined use of probiotic and prebiotic produces a synergistic effect that cannot be achieved with either component alone.[[pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC11148295/)]

## Role of the Probiotic

_Saccharomyces boulardii_ consists of live yeast cells that perform several key functions in the gut.

- **Antimicrobial action**: _S. boulardii_ suppresses pathogenic bacteria, including _E. coli_ and _S. typhi_, which cause acute diarrhea.[[vw-ingredients](https://vw-ingredients.com/ru/%D0%BD%D0%BE%D0%B2%D0%BE%D1%81%D1%82%D0%B8/saccharomyces-boulardii-%D0%BF%D1%80%D0%BE%D0%B1%D0%B8%D0%BE%D1%82%D0%B8%D0%BA-%D0%B4%D0%BB%D1%8F-%D0%B7%D0%B4%D0%BE%D1%80%D0%BE%D0%B2%D1%8C%D1%8F-%D1%87%D0%B5%D0%BB%D0%BE%D0%B2%D0%B5%D0%BA%D0%B0)]
    
- **Anti-inflammatory effect**: The combination of _S. boulardii_ with FOS shows better anti-inflammatory results than _S. boulardii_ alone.[[pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC11148295/)]
    
- **Immunomodulation**: The probiotic regulates immune response, acting either as an immunostimulant or by reducing pro-inflammatory reactions.[[cyberleninka](https://cyberleninka.ru/article/n/klinicheskaya-effektivnost-preparatov-na-osnove-probioticheskih-shtammov-saccharomyces-boulardii)]
    
- **Diarrhea prevention**: Proven efficacy in treating and preventing acute diarrhea, antibiotic-associated diarrhea, and _Clostridium difficile_ recurrence.[[saccharomycesboulardii](https://www.saccharomycesboulardii.com/chto-takoe-saharomicety-bulardi-cncm-i-745/nauchnye-dannye/)]
    

Systematic reviews confirm: _S. boulardii_ is a clinically effective **gut probiotic** with a broad spectrum of action.[[saccharomycesboulardii](https://www.saccharomycesboulardii.com/chto-takoe-saharomicety-bulardi-cncm-i-745/nauchnye-dannye/)]

## Role of the Prebiotic

FOS (fructooligosaccharides) is a prebiotic that is not digested by the animal but serves as a nutrient medium for beneficial bacteria.

- **Nutrient medium**: FOS is utilized by normal microflora, stimulating its growth and biological activity.[[feedconsult](https://feedconsult.ru/news/pro-i-prebiotiki-i-ikh-rol-v-zhivotnovodstve/)]
    
- **Growth of bifidobacteria and lactobacilli**: FOS promotes the increase of friendly gut bacteria populations.[[ga-petfoodpartners](https://ga-petfoodpartners.ru/knowledge-centre/the-importance-of-digestive-health-for-pets/)]
    
- **Improved calcium absorption**: FOS enhances calcium absorption in the lower intestine, supporting bone strength and dental health in dogs.[[ru.sdshinehealth](https://ru.sdshinehealth.com/industry-news/why-your-dog-needs.html)]
    
- **Intestinal acidification**: The prebiotic lowers intestinal pH, suppressing pathogenic microorganism growth.[[feedconsult](https://feedconsult.ru/news/pro-i-prebiotiki-i-ikh-rol-v-zhivotnovodstve/)]
    

**FOS for cats and dogs** is a safe prebiotic that is not digested but supports a healthy microbiome.[[ru.sdshinehealth](https://ru.sdshinehealth.com/industry-news/why-your-dog-needs.html)]

## Why They Work Better Together

The **probiotic and prebiotic for animals** combination creates a synergistic effect.

1. **Synergy of action**
    
    - The combined FOS/_S. boulardii_ approach yields better anti-inflammatory effects and regulates microbiota more effectively than either component alone.[[pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC11148295/)]
        
    - FOS serves as "fuel" for beneficial bacteria, including _S. boulardii_.
        

2. **Enhanced probiotic survival**
    
    - The prebiotic creates a favorable environment for probiotic colonization.
        
    - FOS protects the probiotic from the harsh gastric environment and increases its survival in the gut.[[feedconsult](https://feedconsult.ru/news/pro-i-prebiotiki-i-ikh-rol-v-zhivotnovodstve/)]
        

3. **Comprehensive microbiome impact**
    
    - The probiotic introduces beneficial bacteria from outside.
        
    - The prebiotic stimulates the growth of the animal's own beneficial microflora.[[ga-petfoodpartners](https://ga-petfoodpartners.ru/knowledge-centre/the-importance-of-digestive-health-for-pets/)]
        

4. **Rapid recovery from diarrhea**
    
    - Clinical data show that **S. boulardii FOS** provides rapid support for acute diarrhea in cats and dogs.[[animalbiome](https://animalbiome.vet/products/s-boulardii-fos-powder)]
        
    - The combination improves stool consistency, reduces diarrhea, and supports intestinal function.[[animalbiome](https://animalbiome.vet/products/s-boulardii-fos-powder)]
