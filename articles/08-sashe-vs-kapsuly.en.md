## Why Sachets Are More Convenient Than Capsules for Cats and Dogs

Probiotics based on _Saccharomyces boulardii_ are actively used in veterinary medicine for treating and preventing diarrhea, improving digestion, and supporting immunity in cats and dogs. Among various dosage forms, **S. boulardii sachets** — individual portioned packets of powder — are becoming increasingly common and are significantly more convenient than capsules for pets.

Keywords: _S. boulardii sachet, probiotic sachet for animals, convenient probiotic form, powder for cats and dogs_

---

## Dosing Accuracy

**S. boulardii sachet** provides precise dosing, which is critical for probiotic efficacy.

- Each packet contains a strictly defined number of CFU (typically 7.5×10⁹ CFU per 2.5 g of powder).[[laakari](https://www.laakari.biz/product/boulardii-animals/)]
    
- Capsules contain a fixed human dose not adapted to the animal's body weight.
    
- Systematic reviews show that _S. boulardii_ efficacy directly depends on the accurate dose calculated for the species and weight of the animal.[[animalbiome](https://www.animalbiome.com/products/animalbiome-s-boulardii-fos-powder)]
    

Veterinary sources confirm: incorrect dosing leads to underdosing (no effect) or overdose (excessive fermentation, diarrhea).[[animalbiome](https://animalbiome.vet/products/s-boulardii-fos-powder)]

## Mixing with Food

**Powder for cats and dogs** in sachets mixes easily with food, which is especially important for picky pets.

- The powder distributes evenly in food without altering taste or consistency.[[4lapy](https://4lapy.ru/journal/info/probiotiki-dlya-koshek/)]
    
- **Probiotic sachet for animals** does not require opening capsules and dividing contents "by eye."
    
- Animals are more willing to eat food with powder than to take capsules forcibly.[[ecohimtech](https://ecohimtech.ru/events/publication/probiotiki-dlya-koshek-zachem-oni-nuzhny-i-kak-vybrat-luchshie/)]
    

Capsules, on the other hand, require:

- opening;
    
- extracting the powder;
    
- mixing without guarantee of even distribution.
    

The **convenient probiotic form** in sachets saves the owner time and improves treatment compliance.

## Fewer Application Errors

Using **S. boulardii sachets** minimizes the errors typical of capsules.

1. **Uneven dosage**
    
    - In capsules, the powder is distributed unevenly; CFU cannot be accurately divided.
        
    - A sachet is a ready portion, dosed by the manufacturer.[[laakari](https://www.laakari.biz/product/boulardii-animals/)]
        

2. **Contamination**
    
    - Opening a capsule increases contamination risk.
        
    - Sachets are sealed, maintaining sterility until use.[[4lapy](https://4lapy.ru/journal/info/probiotiki-dlya-koshek/)]
        

3. **Loss of viability**
    
    - _S. boulardii_ is sensitive to humidity and temperature.
        
    - An opened capsule quickly loses activity; a sachet is protected until opening.[[animalbiome](https://www.animalbiome.com/products/animalbiome-s-boulardii-fos-powder)]
        

4. **Lack of veterinary validation for capsules**
    
    - Human capsules are not tested on animals.
        
    - Sachets are developed specifically for cats and dogs, with veterinary approval.[[animalbiome](https://animalbiome.vet/products/s-boulardii-fos-powder)]
        

Veterinary journals emphasize: a **convenient probiotic form** reduces administration errors and improves treatment efficacy.[[animalbiome](https://animalbiome.vet/products/s-boulardii-fos-powder)]
