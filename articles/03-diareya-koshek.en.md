## Diarrhea in Cats

Diarrhea in cats is not a diagnosis but a symptom that can accompany diet changes, stress, parasitic infections, intestinal infections, inflammatory bowel disease, and consequences of antibiotic therapy. Therefore, for prolonged or severe diarrhea, it is important to identify the cause rather than relying solely on supportive measures.[[pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC9414249/)]

## Why S. boulardii Is Used Here

**Saccharomyces boulardii** is a yeast probiotic used to support gut health in animals. Scientific reviews associate it with reduced diarrhea severity, support of the intestinal mucosal barrier, and decreased inflammatory response.[[pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC7344949/)]

For cats, this is particularly helpful in situations where diarrhea is linked to stress, diet change, or antibiotics. Unlike bacterial probiotics, S. boulardii is a yeast and is therefore not directly suppressed by antibiotics, allowing concurrent use.[[animalbiome](https://animalbiome.vet/pages/relieve-the-microbiome)]

## Why FOS Is Added

**FOS** (fructooligosaccharides) is a prebiotic fiber that serves as a nutrient source for beneficial microbiota. Prebiotics help create conditions in which the intestinal ecosystem recovers more sustainably.[[buddydinner](https://www.buddydinner.ru/articles/pomoshch/probiotiki-prebiotiki-i-postbiotiki-kogda-ikh-primenyaem-i-kak-oni-pomogayut/)]

In practical protocols, FOS is added not instead of a probiotic but alongside it: the probiotic helps shift the microbial balance, while the prebiotic supports the growth of beneficial bacteria and improves the overall gut environment. This is why the combination of S. boulardii + FOS is often considered more logical than either component alone.[[animalbiome](https://www.animalbiome.com/products/animalbiome-s-boulardii-fos-powder)]

## Why the Combination Works Better

The S. boulardii + FOS combination is beneficial for three reasons:

- S. boulardii helps with acute and antibiotic-associated diarrhea.[[animalbiome](https://animalbiome.vet/pages/relieve-the-microbiome)]
    
- FOS supports beneficial microbiota and intestinal balance.[[buddydinner](https://www.buddydinner.ru/articles/pomoshch/probiotiki-prebiotiki-i-postbiotiki-kogda-ikh-primenyaem-i-kak-oni-pomogayut/)]
    
- Together, they can simultaneously reduce symptoms and create conditions for gut recovery.[[animalbiome](https://www.animalbiome.com/products/animalbiome-s-boulardii-fos-powder)]
    

For the owner, this means a more practical approach: not just "stopping the diarrhea" but supporting the restoration of intestinal function.[[animalbiome](https://www.animalbiome.com/products/animalbiome-s-boulardii-fos-powder)]

## What the Literature Shows

In animal reviews, S. boulardii is described as a promising probiotic for feed and veterinary applications, especially for intestinal disorders and stress conditions.[[pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC12606370/)]  
Materials referencing scientific data on canine and feline veterinary medicine specifically highlight its role in antibiotic-associated diarrhea and inflammatory bowel conditions.[[animalbiome](https://animalbiome.vet/pages/relieve-the-microbiome)]  
For FOS in a veterinary context, microbiota support and reduction of gastrointestinal and dermatological symptom severity are noted.[[animalbiome](https://animalbiome.vet/pages/relieve-the-microbiome)]

## When the Combination Is Particularly Appropriate

The S. boulardii + FOS combination is most often appropriate for:

- mild to moderate diarrhea in cats;
    
- post-antibiotic diarrhea;
    
- diet change;
    
- stress episodes;
    
- recovery after an intestinal disorder.[[pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC9414249/)]
    

If the cat has blood in the stool, dehydration, vomiting, marked lethargy, or diarrhea lasting more than 1–2 days, veterinary diagnostics are needed because the probiotic does not address the underlying cause.[[pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC7344949/)]

## Summary

**S. boulardii + FOS** is a sensible combination for supporting cats with diarrhea: S. boulardii helps stabilize the gut, while FOS supports the restoration of beneficial microbiota.[[animalbiome](https://www.animalbiome.com/products/animalbiome-s-boulardii-fos-powder)]  
This regimen is most convincingly supported as an aid for stress-related, antibiotic-associated, and functional diarrhea, but not as a substitute for diagnostic workup when severe symptoms are present.[[pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC9414249/)]
